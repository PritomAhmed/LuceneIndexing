If your group is not on the list, please contact the GSC Treasurer. Note that only MIT funded student groups are eligible for the GSC funding. Check your student group’s class and privileges in the ASA database. Applications can be found here.  Funding and Publicity Guidelines  All funded graduate student groups are required to meet the Funding Guidelines. Furthermore, graduate student groups are required to meet our Publicity Guidelines by publicizing their events in the MIT events calendar and in the Anno. Events Calendar and GSC Anno To facilitate event planning and ensure that events sponsored by the GSC are open to all graduate students, please remember to add events to the MIT’s events calendar and the GSC Anno at least two weeks in advance, and include the GSC Funding Board as a co-sponsor. The calendar and anno listing is a required part of the reimbursement request.
