Academics ? Off Campus Studies Off Campus Studies All undergraduate students in the College of Architecture are required to spend one semester away from College Station. This requirement may be fulfilled in or out of the United States. International Options The Study Abroad Program is one option for students choosing to spend their semester way in Europe. This program allows students to spend a full semester with a College of Architecture faculty member at one of three institutions in Santa Chiara, Italy; Barcelona, Spain; or Dusseldorf, Germany Students may also register as a non-degree-seeking student at the international university of their choice. Before pursuing this option, students should speak with their advisor. Domestic Options For students wishing or choosing to stay in the United States, they may fulfill their semester away requirement by taking an internship, or by registering as a non-degree-seeking student at the domestic University of their choice. © 2015 College of Architecture, Texas A&M University.
