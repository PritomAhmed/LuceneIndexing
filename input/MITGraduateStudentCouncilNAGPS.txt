NAGPS acts as a clearinghouse for graduate and professional student organizations, serves as an advocacy group for graduate-professional student needs and rights at all levels, and addresses a broad range of issues via our national committees such as international student concerns, human diversity concerns, legislative concerns, and employment concerns. Every fall, NAGPS hosts a national conference, and every spring, NAGPS member schools meet at regional conferences.
