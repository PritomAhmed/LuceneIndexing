Founded in 1983 by faculty at MIT and Berkeley, Places is a leading journal of contemporary architecture, landscape, and urbanism. Open to: the general public Cost: 10/5/free to MIT ID This event occurs daily through September 7, 2015, except July 4, 2015, September 7, 2015 and May 25, 2015.
