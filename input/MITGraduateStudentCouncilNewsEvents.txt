In addition to The Graduate, our monthly newsletter dedicated to publishing news, opinions, and advocacy information related to graduate students at MIT, we include recents news and upcoming events on our website. As the largest funding body for graduate student events, the GSC has constructed an events calendar aggregating all the graduate student events from across campus. Through our publication resources like The Graduate and The Anno, our weekly event announcement e-mail and our online calendars, we are strive to keep students informed and up-to-date with news and events relating to graduate students.
