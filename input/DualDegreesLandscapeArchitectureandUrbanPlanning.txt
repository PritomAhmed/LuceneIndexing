Dual Degrees Dual Degrees Seven (7) dual degrees are formally offered by the Department of Landscape Architecture and Urban Planning, students with strong motivation to work towards obtaining a dual degree can make arrangements at the beginning of their degree program. Interested students should contact the Program Coordinators of each program listed below as soon as they are admitted for studies.
