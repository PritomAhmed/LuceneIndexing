Facilities Facilities The Offices, Classrooms, and Studios The Department of Landscape Architecture and Urban Planning facilities are located primarily in Building A of the Langford Architecture Center. The Department's main office is located on the third floor in room 311, while the offices of its professors can be found throughout the buildings of the College of Architecture. Technical Reference Center (TRC) Langford A also houses the College's library known as the Technical Reference Center. This library is supported by the College and is not a branch of the University Library System. Its collection contains thousands of materials (books, periodicals, plans, slides, etc.) related to the subject areas covered by the College. Computing The College provides computer labs for the use of its students: one general computing labs on the first floor of Langford A, blue and red pods on the 3rd floor of Langford A, gold and brown pods on the 4th floor of Langford A and a GIS lab on the third floor of Langford A. University-provided wireless networking is available in the College buildings. Computing help can be found at the Help Desk on the first floor of Langford A in room 122. © 2015 College of Architecture, Texas A&M University. Contact Information | Accessibility | Site Policies
