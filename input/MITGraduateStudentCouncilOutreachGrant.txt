It encourages students to go out their classrooms and labs to explore alternative resource on academic and professional development. This grant was developed to provide individual or teams of graduate students with… Passion on improving MIT’s academic and professional development Clear implementable project ideas preferably with lasting impact Objectives: Encourage innovative ideas to promote MIT’s academic environment and students’ professional skills Benefit overall MIT community Make a lasting impact Application Process: Proposal containing a clear, concise plan of project, timeline, and budget E-mail Proposal to gsc-arc-outreach[a]mit[dot]edu Application are reviewed on rolling base during regular semesters. If you have any questions, please email Monika Avello at gsc-arc-outreach[at]mit[dot]edu.
